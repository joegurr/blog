Ordinal and Cardinal Numbers

January 2022

Right now I know next to nothing about the ordinal or Cardinal numbers. I am posting this article to serve as an impetus for me to learn about them and post something soon!

Ordinal numbers tell us an item's position in a list, for example: first, second, third, fourth, etc.

Cardinal Numbers tells us the size of the list, it is a measure of magnitude.
