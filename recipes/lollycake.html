Lolly Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of malt biscuits
  • 1 cup of fruit puffs
  • 3 big tablespoons of melted nuttelex
  • 1/2 a can of coconut condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut

Method

  1. Blend biscuits, chop lollies
  2. Add all ingredients to a mixing bowl, mix well
  3. Press into a tray, sprinkle shredded coconut on the top
  4. Set in the fridge for at least an hour, then you're good to go
